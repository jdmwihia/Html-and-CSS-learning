JOLLOF AND JERK CHICKEN RECIPE

Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and a blend of spices. Here's a simple recipe:

  1. Ingredients:
    • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
    • 1 cup of tomato sauce
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
    • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
    • 1 teaspoon of thyme
    • 1 teaspoon of paprika
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  2. Instructions:
    1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot.
    2. Saut√© the onion, garlic, and peppers until they are soft.
    3. Add the tomato sauce, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 10 minutes.
    4. Stir in the rice and add enough water to cover it. Cook until the rice is tender.
    5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Jerk Chicken

Jerk chicken is a spicy and flavorful dish from Jamaica. Here's a simple recipe:

  1. Ingredients:
    • 4 chicken thighs
    • 2 tablespoons of jerk seasoning
    • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
    • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  2. Instructions:
    1. In a bowl, mix the jerk seasoning, soy sauce, garlic, and olive oil to make a marinade.
    2. Coat the chicken thighs with the marinade and let them marinate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results.
    3. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
    4. Grill the chicken for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
    5. Serve with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!
